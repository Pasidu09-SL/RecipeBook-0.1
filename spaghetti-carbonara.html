Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Eggs
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook bacon or pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and immediately toss with egg mixture.
  5. Add cooked bacon or pancetta and toss until well combined.
  6. Serve hot with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
