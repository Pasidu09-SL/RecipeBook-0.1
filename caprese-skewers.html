Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Fresh mozzarella balls
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball onto each skewer.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
